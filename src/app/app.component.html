Cabins

Ohana is a fully functional summer camp, in fact camp starts the day after we leave! All activities and events will take place on-site, and those staying at the camp will have everything within walking distance. Travel

From NJ and NYC - Est. 4h 30min (280 miles)

Starting in NJ, take NJ-4E to I-95 N. Use the right two lanes to take Exit #1 C-D for Albany. Head North toward Albany. Merge onto I-87 N. On NY I-87 N, take Exit #4 toward Cross County Pkwy E. Merge onto Central Park Ave. Keep left at the fork, follow signs for Hutchinson Pkwy and merge onto Cross County Pkwy/New York State Reference Rte 907K. Merge onto Hutchinson River Pkway N. Keep right at fork on Hutchinson Pkwy N (Entering Connecticut). Continue onto CT-15 N. In 37.6 miles, keep left to stay on CT-15N. Take Exit #68 N-E to merge onto I-91 N toward CT-66 E/Hartford/Middletown (Entering Massachusetts). Stay on I-91N and follow signs for Interstate 91 Greenfield (Entering Vermont). Take Exit #14 for VT-113 toward US-5/Thetford. Follow VT 113 West and 5 Corners Rd to Quinbeck Rd.

From Burlington, VT - 1hr 40 min (77 miles)

Take I-89 S to VT-63 E in Berlin. Take Exit #6 for VT-63 toward VT-14/Barre/So Barre. Continue on VT-63 E. Turn right on Miller Rd Ext. Turn left onto W Rd. (Yes it's actually called "W" Rd, will turn into Falls Bridge) Continue onto Falls Bridge Rd. Turn right onto VT-14 S. Turn left onto Chelsea Rd. Slight left onto Chelsea Rd/Williamstown Rd. Turn right onto VT-110 S. Turn left onto VT-113 E. Turn left onto VT-244 E. Slight right onto Robinson Hill Rd. Turn left onto Quinbeck Rd. Destination will be on the left.
